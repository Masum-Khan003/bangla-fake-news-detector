Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Bangla Fake News Detector ("the Extension") is a browser extension that helps users identify potentially fake news articles in Bangla and English.

What data we collect

When you use the Extension to analyze an article, the text content of that article is sent to our classification API hosted on Railway. We collect:

  • Article text submitted for analysis
  • The URL of the page being analyzed (for caching purposes)
  • Optional feedback (thumbs up/down) if you choose to submit it

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information
  • We do not track your browsing history
  • We do not sell any data to third parties
  • We do not use cookies or advertising trackers

How data is used

Article text is sent to our API solely for the purpose of classification. Prediction results are cached for 1 hour to improve performance. Optional feedback submissions are stored to improve model accuracy in future versions.

Data retention

Cached predictions are automatically deleted after 1 hour. Feedback submissions are retained for model improvement purposes and are not linked to any individual user.

Third-party services

The Extension communicates with our API hosted on Railway. The trained model is hosted on HuggingFace. No other third-party services receive your data.

Open source

The full source code for this extension and its backend API is available on GitHub.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
